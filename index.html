FBI Colluded with Child Kidnappers and Tried to Murder the Father

Sun, 09 Mar 2024

Rev. Balfour’s Five-Year-Old Daughter Remains Missing

Lieutenant Hank Pond attempted to kill Rev. Balfour, the reputed priest of the Church of Passover Angel and the father of a kidnapped ailing girl, Regina Balfour. Regina was abducted by two confirmed suspects: George Lewiston, a retired ATF investigator, and Lindsay Peale, a child welfare social worker.

Despite the failure in finding the missing girl, Pond and three FBI agents secretly broken into Balfour’s house, held Rev. Balfour at gunpoint, and attempted to decapitate Ms. Balfour. All were done without a warrant. They also moved the Lewiston family out of Ohio without prior notice to Cleveland PD. An involved agent, Joshua Peale, is a sibling of Lindsay Peale, the aforementioned kidnapper.

One of the agents, Leanne Lee, once said to a local police captain, "Sir, I'm the intel officer. If you know as much as I do, then how come it's called INTEL?" As reported yesterday, Lee spied on the Balfour family and secretly installed listening devices on Rev. Balfour's personal vehicle.

"They showed little respect to our small church who dedicates all we have to the local community's welfare," Reverend Balfour said. "We don't feel safe anymore with our daughter missing, our car and home spied upon, and our religious practices and personal security dismissed. All that reminds us of a year ago, when our daughter was torn away from us by local institutions... It’s blatant persecution. Their endless abuse of power is appaling... How can our lives and rights ever be guaranteed?”

Ms. Balfour is acquainted with one of the FBI agents implicated, yet refused to reveal the agent's identity.

The community of Cleveland stands in solidarity with the Balfour family, demanding answers and justice for Regina. Church members are organizing vigils and protests to draw attention to the alleged FBI misconduct and to implore them to prioritize the safe return of the innocent child.

Our journalists will closely follow this ongoing investigation, providing updates as new details come to light. Our thoughts are with the Balfour family during this incredibly difficult time, and we join the Cleveland community in seeking the truth and holding those responsible accountable for their actions.
